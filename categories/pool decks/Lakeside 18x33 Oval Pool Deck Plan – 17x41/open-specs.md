## Open Features

- Large oval pool opening (approx. 18×33 ft pool designed separately by others)
- 6×6 posts (SP No.2) on 18×18×12 in concrete footings
- Beams: (2) 2×12 SP No.2 built-up beams
- Joists: 2×6 SP No.2 @ 12 in O.C. with Simpson H1A hurricane ties
- Decking: 2×6 composite decking boards with 1/8 in gap
- Wraparound railing with 4×4 posts @ 4 ft O.C.
- Guardrails: 2×4 rails with 2×2 balusters
- Dual stair access with 2×12 stair stringers @ 12 in O.C.
- Stair footings: 48×24×4 in concrete landing pads
- Blocking between joists for added structural stability
